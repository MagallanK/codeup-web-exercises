My favorite Movies and Shows:

American Sniper
A movie which shows the story of the Navy Seal Chris Kyle.
During the end of the movie there is a collage of his services taking place at the Dallas Cowboys stadium, which myself and some others from my platoon were able to be there in support of all he accomplished.
John Wick 1,2,3
A movie about an organization of 'assassins' that take part in completing contracts around the world where one of the assassins John Wick was able to get out after a 'suicide mission' he did to get married and settled down.
That all changes once his wife passes, and she leaves him with a surprise of a puppy to help him move on, and during his recovery phase, he chose to get back into the business he left and seek out those who did him wrong when they took the gift his wife left for him away.
Dragon Ball Z
An anime show revolving around the character Goku (Kakarot) who is a fighter on Earth that continually fights against people who are trying to destroy or take away those he most cares about.
During the later part of the show Goku learns about where he initially came from and the Saiyan race that he is part of and continues to learn more about himself and protect the Earth and his loved ones from those who seek him out and want to take everything from him.
My Hero Academia
An anime show where 80% of earths population has 'quirks' or super-powers that the people use to either be hero's or villains.
The story revolves around one boy who was 'quirkless' and always wanted to be a hero, but without a quirk which develops at a young age, you cannot be a hero since you have no quirk. This young boy is given a quirk by his most admired hero and is able to now go through high school at UA High, which helps students who have quirks develop them further to become proper hero's.

DragonBall Z Facts:

  • The main character is Goku a Saiyan, a warrior race.
  • Vegeta is Goku's rival, the next main character, also a Saiyan.
  • Vegeta started out as villain, then became good.
  • There are higher power levels known as Super Saiyan.

My Hero Academia Facts:

  • The main character is Izuku Midoriya, who started out with no power.
  • Izuku Midoriya's hero name is Deku.
  • Deku was given his quirk by All Might the #1 hero.
  • Deku learns to control his new quirk in order to become a hero.

American Sniper Facts:

  • The movie is based off the true story about Navy Seal Chris Kyle.
  • Chris Kyle after his time in service moved on to help other soldiers with PTSD.
  • Chris Kyle was killed when trying to help another veteran.
  • Chris Kyle was one of the top snipers in the military.
